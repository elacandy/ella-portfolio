i was commissioned to create this piece for ella jade’s father, inspired by his favourite movie and the original book cover. each green face i drew represents a patient entangled in a chaotic system. the single red club rises above them, breaking free. the hand-drawn title echoes old typewriter text, grounding it in the era the story comes from.

under pressure

i work best under pressure, keeping sharp focus and pouring deep care into every detail. even when things feel chaotic, i’m able to deliver with clarity and a calm mind. i hold myself to the highest standards and rarely let anything slide past my own critique.

burnout happens sometimes, but i use it as fuel to push forward. it teaches me resilience, sharpens my drive, and reminds me that i’m always evolving through the work i create. each challenge becomes part of my growth, shaping not only my craft but the way i approach every new project.

wildcare canada - brand book

the overarching goal of this project was to establish the foundational information architecture for a new website for wildcare canada, a dedicated wildlife rehabilitation centre. this involved developing a strategy for a usable, functional, and desirable website.

information architecture and ux strategy for wildcare canada. a (fictional) platform designed to educate, inspire, and drive donations for wildlife rehabilitation.

focused on raising awareness, streamlining donations, growing community support, and building long-term sustainability through clear navigation, strong content structure, and a user-first approach.

through user research and persona development, the site was tailored to real audience needsâ€”balancing depth with clarity. competitive analysis and concept mapping informed a smart, intuitive structure, supported by facet filters and clear labeling. the design system featured a serif/sans-serif type combo, earthy colours, and accessible ui elements. the project moved from wireframes to wordpress, with seo, analytics, and functionality built in from the start.
